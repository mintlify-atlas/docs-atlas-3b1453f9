WZRD Studio is a macOS desktop application that unifies every step of an AI-powered video production workflow. Start from a concept, generate a full storyboard with AI imagery and video, assemble a Director's Cut, refine it in a multi-track editor, and publish across social channels — all without leaving the app. A built-in agent layer lets AI assistants drive any part of that workflow on your behalf via MCP, voice commands, or the editor command API.

## The WZRD Workflow

WZRD Studio organizes creative production into a linear pipeline that you can enter at any stage.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up a project">
    Create a project from the Home dashboard. Define your concept, format, and characters. WZRD generates storyline variations for you to choose from.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate your storyboard">
    The Storyboard page turns your storyline into scenes and shots. Trigger AI image and video generation per shot, or run the full timeline with a single click.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assemble with Director's Cut">
    Director's Cut automatically assembles generated shots into a final video, applies transitions, and outputs a downloadable asset — no manual timeline work required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit and refine">
    Open the QCut-powered multi-track Video Editor or Clip Studio for FFmpeg-backed trimming, captions, effects, and export. Agents can drive edits via the editor command API.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish with Postz">
    Schedule and publish your finished content across connected social channels from the Postz calendar. Attach media from your project assets directly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
